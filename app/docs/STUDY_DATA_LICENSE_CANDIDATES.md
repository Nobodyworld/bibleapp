# Study Data License Candidates (Draft)

## Scope

Draft research list only. This file does not change packaged data policy or the official retained-data matrix.

## Review Questions To Answer For Every Source

- Can this dataset be redistributed publicly?
- Can this dataset be used commercially?
- Can it be sold bundled with the app?
- Is attribution required?
- Does modification require notice?

## Candidate Categories and Source Leads

### Commentaries

Potential sources:

- Public-domain commentary corpora (source-by-source review required).
- Provider APIs or licensed modern commentary datasets.

Exact license questions:

- Are full commentary texts redistributable as bundled app data?
- Are excerpt-only indexes treated differently from full text?
- Are there territory restrictions?
- Is commercial resale of bundled commentary content allowed?

### Cross References

Potential sources:

- Open/public cross-reference datasets.
- Curated references from publishers with explicit reuse terms.

Exact license questions:

- Are reference link graphs copyright-protected compilations?
- Is redistribution of generated cross-reference indexes allowed?
- Is attribution placement mandatory in-app or in docs only?

### Footnotes

Potential sources:

- Footnotes attached to translations with permissive terms.
- Independently licensed annotation sets.

Exact license questions:

- Are footnotes derivative of protected translation text?
- Are footnote markers and offsets redistributable separately?
- Can edited/normalized footnotes be redistributed?

### Strong's and Lexicon

Potential sources:

- Public-domain Strong's lexical datasets.
- Enhanced lexical entries from third-party providers.

Exact license questions:

- Is Strong's index data itself unrestricted or source-restricted?
- Are lexical glosses/definitions in the source under copyright?
- Is commercial bundling of lexical text allowed?

### Morphology

Potential sources:

- Public-domain morphology annotations.
- License-required morphology layers tied to source texts.

Exact license questions:

- Can token-level morphology tags be redistributed independently?
- Are source-token alignments considered derivative works?
- Is commercial packaging permitted for modified tags?

### Interlinear

Potential sources:

- Interlinear alignments from public-domain source stacks.
- Publisher-licensed alignments.

Exact license questions:

- Are alignment tables redistributable even when linked to copyrighted translations?
- Is clause required for attribution near each verse or in global notice?
- Is resale/bundling allowed in paid distributions?

### Outlines

Potential sources:

- Public-domain outline structures.
- Editorial outlines from licensed publishers.

Exact license questions:

- Are outline hierarchies copyrightable editorial content?
- Is redistribution allowed for modified section labels?
- Must attribution be retained at per-book level?

### Search and Derived Indexes

Potential sources:

- Generated indexes from approved source data.

Exact license questions:

- Do source licenses permit redistribution of derivative search indexes?
- Can keyword and posting-list indexes be sold in bundled form?
- Must provenance and modification notes be shipped with derived indexes?

1. Cross references
2. Footnotes
3. Outlines
4. Search indexes
5. Strong's and lexicon
6. Interlinear and morphology

This order prioritizes high-utility, lower-cognitive-load study features nearest to reader flow.
