# Retained Data Licenses

This app keeps only text editions that are public domain or have no license requirement according to the cited upstream notice.

- BSB, Berean Study Bible: Berean Bible states the Berean Bible and Majority Bible texts were placed into the public domain as of April 30, 2023. Source: https://berean.bible/licensing.htm
- WEB, World English Bible: eBible.org states the World English Bible is in the public domain. Source: https://ebible.org/eng-web/copyright.htm
- KJV, ASV, ERV, DBT, DRB, SLT, WBT, YLT: retained as public-domain historical English translations in the United States.

Removed datasets should not be restored without adding source-specific license/provenance review.
